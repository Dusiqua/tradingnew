About Me

Introduction

Born in 2005, I am an ambitious forex trader with a strong academic foundation in Business Administration, which I am currently studying at the renowned Vienna University of Economics and Business (WU Vienna).

I began actively engaging with international currency markets at the age of 15. Since then, I have developed a solid understanding of technical analysis, risk management, and trading psychology - always with the goal of trading systematically and based on data.

I live and work between Budapest and Vienna, which provides me with unique insights into Central European economies and markets. My focus lies in analyzing macroeconomic developments and combining them with precise technical setups in both intraday and swing trading.

My mission is to achieve sustainable success in the forex market through transparency, discipline, and continuous improvement - both in independent trading and in collaboration with partners like FundedNext, who value real expertise and proven results.

My Strategy

My trading strategy is primarily based on price action. I focus on interpreting raw market movement without relying on indicators, allowing me to react directly to what the market is showing in real time.

Trading with Smart Money: A key element of my approach is the use of support and resistance zones - identifying where price has historically reacted and using these levels to anticipate potential turning points or breakouts.

This strategy provides a clean, logical structure for my trades and helps maintain a disciplined, objective view of market behavior.
